# Research program

## Purpose

Sequence the evidence work required to create reliable audience, Performance & Reward, consulting, visual-design, dashboard UX, and Power BI knowledge packs.

## What this file must establish

- Research waves and dependency order
- Research questions and expected outputs for each wave
- Required source classes and freshness requirements
- Parallel work that can be isolated safely
- Review and synthesis gates
- How current-product research is refreshed
- How proprietary and licensed material is handled
- How research findings become knowledge packs, skills, and evaluations

## Required outputs

- Workstream map
- Prioritised research backlog
- Source plan by domain
- Research-record assignments
- Review cadence
- Stop and rework conditions
- Completion measures

## Acceptance criteria

- Foundational definitions are researched before specialist skills depend on them
- Research work avoids duplicate coverage and conflicting authorities
- Current-product and regulatory content has explicit refresh triggers
- Each workstream has a decision or capability use
- The program supports staged delivery rather than attempting the full catalogue at once
